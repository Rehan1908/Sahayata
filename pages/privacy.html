Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Your privacy and well-being are our top priorities.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Name and email for registration
  • Anonymous Content: Posts in Samvad are completely anonymous
  • Journey Progress: Your wellness journey data is encrypted and private
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our platform

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All passwords are hashed and encrypted
  • Personal data is never shared with third parties
  • Anonymous mode allows browsing without any data collection
  • You can delete your account and data at any time

Anonymous Features

Our platform prioritizes anonymity:

  • Samvad posts are completely anonymous
  • No tracking or identification in anonymous areas
  • Browse all content without creating an account

Terms of Service

Acceptable Use

To maintain a safe and supportive community:

  • Be kind and respectful to others
  • No harassment, hate speech, or harmful content
  • Respect confidentiality and privacy
  • Do not share personal identifying information

Mental Health Disclaimer

Important: Sahayata is a peer support platform and not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing a crisis, please contact:

  • Emergency: Call 112 or your local emergency number
  • KIRAN Helpline: 1800-599-0019 (24/7)
  • Your healthcare provider for ongoing mental health support

Account Termination

You may delete your account at any time. We will:

  • Delete all your personal information
  • Maintain anonymous posts for community benefit
  • Provide data export before deletion if requested

Updates to Policy

We may update this policy as our platform evolves. We'll notify users of significant changes and always maintain our commitment to privacy and student well-being.

Contact Us: If you have questions about privacy or need support, please reach out through our platform or contact the Aapatkal helpline.
