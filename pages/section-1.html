The Silent Crisis on Campus

The future of our nation is being shaped within the walls of our colleges. But behind those walls, a silent, devastating crisis is unfolding. This is a mental health epidemic stealing futures at a horrifying rate. Around the world the biggest killer of young people is road accidents. In India, it is suicide.

Those aged 18–45 account for about two‑thirds of suicide deaths. Because of stigma and the criminalization of attempts, many families report deaths as accidents or illnesses. Experts estimate true student suicides may be 2–3× higher.
