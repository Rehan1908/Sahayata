The anatomy of a student's pain

Academic pressure, career anxiety, socio‑cultural expectations, and adjustment challenges create a vicious cycle of stress and silence.

Key stressors and impact

  • Academic pressure: fierce competition, memorization‑heavy syllabi, fear of exams & failure.
  • Financial & career anxiety: rising costs, uncertain jobs, pressure to support family.
  • Socio‑cultural expectations: parental pressure, forced career tracks, constant comparison.
  • Personal adjustment: living away from home, loneliness, hostel transition.

Stigma makes help‑seeking hard. Any solution must be anonymous, confidential, and non‑judgmental.
