Emergency help (Aapatkal)

If you are in danger or thinking about harming yourself, please call now. You are not alone.

Call Tele‑MANAS 24×7

What to do right now

  • Call someone you trust or a helpline. Speaking to a person helps.
  • Remove anything around you that you could use to harm yourself.
  • Try the 60‑second grounding to steady your body and mind.
  • If you’re in a hostel, step into a safer, public space.

60‑second grounding

Inhale gently… 4 counts
Follow the circle as it grows (inhale), holds, and shrinks (exhale).
